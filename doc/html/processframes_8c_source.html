33//
34// In traditional vocal synthesis, the glottal pulse drives filters, which
35// are attenuated to the frequencies of the formants.
36//
37// SAM generates these formants directly with sin and rectangular waves.
38// To simulate them being driven by the glottal pulse, the waveforms are
39// reset at the beginning of each glottal pulse.
40//
